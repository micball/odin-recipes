Grandma's Buttermilk Syrup

Description

This buttermilk syrup comes from Katie Madrian's grandma. It's one of the most delicious syrups you will ever eat. Gauranteed.

This goes especially well on good cinnamon french toast, and even better on a delicious batch of pumpkin waffles.

A word of caution before you make this: use a bigger pan than you think necessary, much bigger. Adding the baking soda causes the syrup to foam a lot, and if your pan isn't large enough, you're going to make a very sticky mess.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup white sugar
  • 1/2 Cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Heat sugar, buttermilk, and butter in a saucepan over medium heat until the mixture starts to boil, about 5 minutes.
  2. Remove saucepan from heat and stir baking soda and vanilla into the buttermilk mixture.
